Teaching

Private lessons for musicians who want clearer ears.

Lessons can cover guitar, composition, arranging, ear training, songwriting or the practical problem of finishing work. The point is not to collect exercises. The point is to hear better and make better decisions.

  • Useful for beginners who want structure.
  • Useful for working musicians who want sharper feedback.
  • Useful for non-musicians who need to speak about music with less panic.

Approach

The process is simple on purpose.

01

Brief

What is fixed, what is open, who is listening and what should change after they hear it.

02

References

Not to copy them. To agree on taste before everyone starts guessing.

03

Version

A clear first pass with notes. Mystery is overrated at this stage.

04

Handoff

Final files, context and decisions documented well enough for the next person.
